Meet Our Strollers

Strol

Compact, yet loaded with comfort and convenience. The streamlined design of the Strol offers more without the extra weight. Glide over bumpy streets with four shock-absorbing wheels, effortlessly navigate crowded spaces with 360-degree front-wheel turning, and carry everything you need with a spacious back pouch and storage basket. Plus, with a patented one-hand folding and unfolding mechanism, you can take the Strol anywhere with confidence!

Stroller specifics: (L x W x H):

  • Size: 33.1" x 20.5" x 41.3"
  • Folded: 11.4" x 20.5" x 21.7"
  • Weight: 16.8lbs net / 19.8lbs gross
  • Wheels (F,R): 6", 7"
  • 40HQ Ship: 880 pcs

About GaiaBaby

At GaiaBaby, we are committed to providing families with the finest quality strollers at affordable prices. We believe that every child deserves the best start in life, and that begins with safe, durable, and stylish products that babies can indulge in and parents can trust and exhibit.

GIVING BACK

We also believe in the power of giving back. As part of our commitment to social responsibility, we pledge 5% of our profits to support moms in need—because no parent should have to choose between quality and affordability.

SUSTAINABILITY

Beyond our dedication to excellence, we are passionate about protecting the environment for future generations. That's why we prioritize sustainability by utilizing the best recycled materials in our manufacturing process, reducing our ecological footprint while maintaining superior craftsmanship.
